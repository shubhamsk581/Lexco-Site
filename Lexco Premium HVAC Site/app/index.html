We recommend having your HVAC system professionally serviced twice a year—once in the spring before cooling season and once in the fall before heating season. Regular maintenance helps prevent breakdowns, improves efficiency, and extends the life of your system.

Common signs include: warm air blowing from vents, weak airflow, unusual noises (grinding, squealing), frequent cycling on and off, high humidity indoors, water leaks around the unit, and unexpectedly high energy bills. If you notice any of these, call Lexco for a diagnostic.
